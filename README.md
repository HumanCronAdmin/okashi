## Why this tool?

People constantly ask "What's a good Japanese cheese snack?" or "Any crunchy Japanese snacks that aren't sweet?" — but no site lets you actually search by flavor and texture. Okashi fills that gap.

## Features

- **Filter by flavor** — matcha, cheese, strawberry, chocolate, and 15+ more
- **Filter by texture** — crispy, crunchy, chewy, soft, mochi, melty
- **Tier list maker** — drag and drop to rank snacks, export as PNG, share on social media
- **Snack personality quiz** — 6 questions, 8 personality types, shareable results
- **Collection tracker** — mark snacks as tried, track your progress with a visual counter
- **35 real snacks** — every item verified from actual products in Japan

## How It Works

1. **Explore** — browse snacks and filter by what matters to you
2. **Play** — create tier lists, take the quiz, discover your snack type
3. **Collect** — track what you've tried and find your next favorite

[Try it live →](https://humancronadmin.github.io/okashi/)

## Privacy

All data stays in your browser (localStorage). No accounts, no server, no tracking beyond anonymous analytics (GA4).
