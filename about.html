About Okashi

Okashi is the first Japanese snack database that lets you search by flavor and texture. Built in Japan, with real data from real snacks.

Why does this exist?

We noticed people constantly asking the same questions on Reddit and social media: "What's a good Japanese cheese snack?" "Any crunchy Japanese snacks that aren't sweet?" "What should I buy at a Japanese convenience store?"

Existing sites sell you subscription boxes or list "Top 10 Japanese Snacks" — but none let you actually search by the things that matter: flavor, texture, and sweetness level.

What makes us different

  • Filter by flavor and texture — no other site does this for Japanese snacks
  • Tier list maker — rank your favorites and share the image
  • Personality quiz — find your snack type and discover new favorites
  • Collection tracker — keep track of everything you've tried
  • Based in Japan — all data is verified from actual products

Built with care

Every snack in our database is a real product with verified information. No AI-generated data, no fake reviews. We buy the snacks, check the details, and add them to the database.

This site is free and open. If you have suggestions, find an error, or want to recommend a snack we should add, we'd love to hear from you.
