We Tested 6 Matcha Snacks — Here's What Our Family Actually Thought

April 2026 · 4 tasters · Tested in Japan

We sat down as a family of four — me, my brother, our mom, and our dad — and taste-tested 6 different matcha snacks you can buy at any Japanese convenience store. No sponsors, no free products. Just honest opinions from people who actually live in Japan and eat these things.

Here's what happened.

Final Rankings

After tasting all 6, each of us picked a top 2.

Taster#1#2
MeBlack Thunder MatchaKitKat Matcha Chocolat
BrotherBlack Thunder MatchaKitKat Rich Matcha
MomKitKat Matcha ChocolatKitKat Rich Matcha
DadKitKat Rich MatchaKitKat Matcha Chocolat

The Verdict

Black Thunder Matcha took the overall win — strong matcha flavor, good crunch, great balance. And it costs about 30 yen. The best value matcha snack in Japan by far.

Both KitKats were close behind and universally liked. If you want pure matcha, go for the Rich Matcha. If you want matcha + chocolate balance, the Matcha Chocolat is the one.

Country Ma'am Matcha was the biggest disappointment. Too sweet, no real matcha flavor. All four of us agreed on that.

If you're visiting Japan and can only grab 1-2 matcha snacks from a konbini, get a Black Thunder Matcha and a KitKat. Skip the Country Ma'am.
