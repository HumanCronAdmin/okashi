Common page structure
========================================

1. lead sentence (with a single word of that brand's "air")
   → Not a history timeline, but the brand's pastries.
     Describe where they are in daily life and what kind of fun they are.

2. discovery ("Did you know?")
   → "Oh, is this the same company as this?
   → Surprising particulars and points of view of that brand
   → Interesting facts that cannot be conveyed on the official website

3. Products (as shown on the website)
   → Each product comes with a one-word comment on the experience
   → Write about the "sensation of eating" rather than the specifications

4. Scene suggestions ("When you reach for it") → Match the confectionery with specific life situations
   → Match specific life situations with the confectionery
   → Make the scene come to mind just by reading it

5. Related article links
   → Go to articles in which the brand's products appear

6. link to the official website (respectfully and unobtrusively)
