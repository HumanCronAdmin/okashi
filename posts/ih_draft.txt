## From Personal Craving to Public Tool: I Built Okashi!, a Japanese Snack Discovery Site (Solo!)

Hey Indie Hackers!

I'm super excited (and a little nervous!) to finally share something I've been pouring my heart and soul into: **Okashi!**

It's a dedicated platform for **Japanese snack enthusiasts** to discover, explore, and share their favorite treats. You can check it out here: **[https://humancronadmin.github.io/okashi/](https://humancronadmin.github.io/okashi/)**

### The "Why": Solving My Own Snack Struggles

As a huge fan of Japanese snacks myself, living abroad, I always ran into the same problem: **how do I find new snacks that match what I *actually* like?**

*   **"I want something chewy, matcha-flavored, and not too sweet."** – Good luck trying to find that on an international e-commerce site!
*   **"What's that crunchy, savory snack I saw on TikTok, but I can't remember the name?"** – Endless scrolling, often fruitless.
*   **"Which Pocky flavor is the *best*?"** – A question that deserves a proper debate tool.

Relying on random Reddit threads or fleeting TikTok videos was a hit-or-miss game. I wanted a structured, fun way to explore the incredible world of Japanese confectionery
